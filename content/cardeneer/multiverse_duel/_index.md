## Overview

 **Multiverse Duel** is an epic strategy card game where players harness the power of countless universes to battle for control of the multiverse. Drawing heavy inspiration from Hearthstone, it offers a familiar yet refreshing twist on the collectible card game genre. Give it a shot!
